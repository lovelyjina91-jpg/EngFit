What's Inside Earth?

Earth is like a giant onion with 4 layers! 🧅

🔴

Inner Core

Solid metal. Super hot — about 5,500°C! Like the surface of the sun!

🟠

Outer Core

Liquid metal. Spinning around the inner core. Creates Earth's magnetic field!

🟡

Mantle

Hot, melted rock called magma. This is what comes out of volcanoes!

🟤

Crust

The thin outer skin of Earth. We live on it! Made of rocks and dirt.

🤔 The crust is the thinnest layer. If Earth were an apple, the crust would be thinner than the apple's skin!
🌋
Volcanoes — Mountains of Fire!

A volcano is an opening in Earth's crust where hot melted rock comes out. When it erupts — WATCH OUT! 💥

VENT (top hole) MAGMA (inside) EARTH'S CRUST
Magma 🔴

Hot melted rock inside Earth.

Lava 🟠

Hot melted rock outside Earth (after eruption).

🌶️ Lava is super hot! About 700 - 1,200°C. That's hot enough to melt steel! Stay far, far away! 🔥

3 Types of Volcanoes

😴

Active

Erupted recently. Could erupt again soon!

😪

Dormant

Sleeping! Hasn't erupted in a long time, but might wake up.

💀

Extinct

Dead! Will never erupt again.

⚡
Earthquakes — Earth Shakes!

An earthquake is a sudden shaking of the ground. It happens when Earth's giant rock pieces (called plates) move and bump into each other! 🤜🤛

⚡ How Earthquakes Work

1

Tectonic Plates Move

Giant pieces of Earth's crust slowly slide and push against each other.

🧩
2

Energy Builds Up

The plates get stuck. Stress and pressure grow over many years.

⚡
3

SNAP! Energy Released

Suddenly the rocks break and slip — sending shock waves everywhere!

💥
4

Ground Shakes

The shock waves travel through the ground. Buildings shake, things fall!

🌊
📊
The Richter Scale

Scientists measure earthquake strength using the Richter Scale. The bigger the number, the stronger the earthquake!

Magnitude Strength What Happens
1 - 3 Tiny You can't even feel it!
4 Small Things shake a little. Windows rattle.
5 - 6 Medium Furniture moves. Some buildings damaged.
7 - 8 Strong Buildings fall. People scared. Big damage!
9+ HUGE Massive destruction! Tsunamis can happen!
🤯 Each number on the scale is 10 times stronger than the one below! A magnitude 6 is 10× stronger than a 5!

of all volcanoes

are located in the "Ring of Fire" — a huge circle around the Pacific Ocean!

90%

of all earthquakes

also happen here! Countries like Japan, Indonesia, and Chile are inside this danger zone.

450+

active volcanoes
